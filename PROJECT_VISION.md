# StudySnap AI

Mission:

Help students convert textbook photos, handwritten notes and PDFs into study materials within seconds.

Core Features:

- Image Upload
- PDF Upload
- AI Summary
- Flashcards
- Quiz Generator
- Study Sessions

Target Users:

- Primary School Students
- High School Students
- University Students

Monetization:

Free:
- 20 uploads/month

Premium:
- Unlimited uploads
- Flashcards
- Quizzes
- AI Tutor Chat

Technology:

- Next.js
- Supabase
- OpenAI
- Vercel
